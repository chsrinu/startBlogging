Recycle your experience by sharing with others, Don't let it go waste!!!  StartBlogging.. 


Blog is one of the way of sharing our thoughts/ experiences/ knowledge to others. From the writer point of view they can be used as a diary to keep a record of one's own achievements and interesting material. Connection with global communities provides insights and networks that can accelerate your learning process, opportunity and success. Today blogging seems to be an amusing technique since it teaches new things everyday to reader and surprisingly the writer as well. Readers definition for blog is..


"__online magazines, books and resources all rolled into one is a blog__"


Creating is one thing, but publishing is another. Being willing to put your ideas “out there” and answer the questions related to your blog takes bravery and a willingness to be vulnerable. Doubts may stop you. These include
  • Why would anyone want to read my ideas?
  • I have nothing new to say.
Overcome your fears and publish your creation.
